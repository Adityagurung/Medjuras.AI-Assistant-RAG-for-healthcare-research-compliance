What each metric means

  • Hit@5 (Hit Rate): Found the right chunk somewhere in the top results
  • MRR@5 (Mean Reciprocal Rank): How high the first correct chunk was ranked
  • MAP@5 (Mean Average Precision): Overall quality of the ranked list
  • nDCG@5 (Normalized Discounted Cumulative Gain): Rewards putting the right chunk nearer the top
